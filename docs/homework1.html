What is Statistics, and Why Is It Useful for Cybersecurity?

Understanding Statistics

Statistics is the science of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data. It helps us understand patterns and trends hidden in large datasets, providing a solid foundation for decision-making under uncertainty.

Relevance in Cybersecurity

In cybersecurity, statistics plays a crucial role in identifying anomalies, detecting intrusions, and assessing risks. By analyzing network traffic, user behavior, or system logs, statistical models can reveal patterns that indicate potential attacks or vulnerabilities.

For example, statistical techniques such as outlier detection can signal unusual activity in a network, while probabilistic models help estimate the likelihood of future threats. These tools allow security experts to respond faster and more effectively.
